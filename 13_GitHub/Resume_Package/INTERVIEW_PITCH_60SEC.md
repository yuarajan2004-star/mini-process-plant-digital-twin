The objective of my project was to build a complete Digital Twin platform for a mini process plant. The system collected pressure, flow and temperature data through an MQTT-based architecture, stored the information in a historian database and displayed it on a real-time dashboard. I then developed a Random Forest predictive maintenance model capable of identifying low pressure, high pressure, low flow, high temperature and combined fault conditions. The project included engineering documentation, FAT, SAT, commissioning and validation activities, achieving a 100% success rate across six test scenarios.
